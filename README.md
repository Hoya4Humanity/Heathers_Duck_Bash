🕹️ Field Instructions: How to Bash 🛠️
The river is chaotic, but a true Basher stays cool. Here is your combat guide:

🎮 The Basics
Movement: Aim your crosshair with the mouse. 🖱️
Fire: Left-click to launch your current projectile. 💥
Survival: You start with 3 Hearts ❤️. Every time a duck escapes the bottom of the screen, you lose a heart. Leveling up restores your health!
Ammunition: Special weapons (TP and Turds) are limited! Refill your stock by defeating bosses and hitting Level Milestones.

🪠 The God-Mode Arsenal
Standard Plunger: Infinite ammo. The hammer of the river. 🔨
TP Spread (Lvl 2): 🧻 Fires 3 streams. Essential for crowd control.
Edibares (Lvl 3): 🧸 Magic gummy bears! These slow enemies to a crawl. Use them on Black Swans!
Turd Bombs (Lvl 4): 💩 Explosive grenades. Huge AOE damage!
Magic Shrooms (Lvl 5): 🍄 Red & White power! Hits cause the Dizzy Effect—enemies spin uncontrollably while stars fly out!

🦢 The Threat Levels
Ducks: Standard grunts. 🦆
Heavy Geese: Tanky and tough. 🦢
The Black Swan: High-speed silhouettes that bounce erratically. 🦢🖤
Mythological Bosses: Giant foes like the Alamo Quacker and Storm-Clucker TX. 🦖⚡
